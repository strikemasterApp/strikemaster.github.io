Privacy Policy for StrikeMaster

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

StrikeMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our bowling tracking iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture when you create an account
  • Bowling Data: Game scores, statistics, bowling ball information, and session data
  • Social Features: Friend connections, activity feed posts, and shared content
  • Profile Information: Avatar images, favorite locations, preferred bowling balls

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your location to find nearby bowling alleys
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and unique device identifiers

Information from Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used to scan bowling scorecards with AI technology (when you explicitly use this feature)
  • Photo Library: Used to upload profile pictures and bowling-related images
  • Location Services: Used to discover nearby bowling alleys and tag game locations

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain bowling tracking services
  • Sync your data across devices via Supabase cloud storage
  • Calculate and display bowling statistics and analytics
  • Enable social features like friend connections and activity feeds
  • Find bowling alleys near your location
  • Process scorecard images with AI (OpenAI Vision API)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important app updates and notifications

Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is securely stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • Local Storage: Data is also stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Authentication: Secure authentication is handled through Supabase Auth

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: For authentication, database storage, and data synchronisation
  • Google Places API: To find and display bowling alley information
  • OpenAI Vision API: To process scorecard images (images are processed and not stored)
  • Apple CloudKit: For iOS-specific features and backups

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under strict confidentiality)

Your Data Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your bowling data
  • Opt-out of specific features (like location services)
  • Control social sharing preferences

Children's Privacy

StrikeMaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts have data removed within 30 days
  • Some anonymised statistical data may be retained for app improvement

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU/EEA

We are committed to protecting your privacy rights under European, UK, and international data protection laws.
